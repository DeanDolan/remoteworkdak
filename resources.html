RESOURCES FOR REMOTE WORK AND STUDY

Remote working and study is a new upcomming concept for many people due covid therfore working in an enclosed space with many people was seen to be unefficiant and dangerous as covid is dying out slowly and more under control some are able to return back to work but it is still a hazard. Many companies are still going to use the rules of only visiting the office once a week and many colleges still offer online lectures as now a few people will be working during the week aswell therfore they can catch up later with recorded lectures.

When working or learning your college course from home it can be difficult. Therefore I will give you some tips and provide some links to help you understand some benefical practices that can help make this so much easier for you and your coworkers to help manage your mental and physical health while working from home. Many find it hard as you dont commute into work that will provide you with a suitable working space therefore when working or studying from home it can be difficult as you may not have a quiet suitable space that can be provided for you.

Some suggestions I have if you cannot find a suitable space for yourself is to either one visit your local or college library that is provided for free to study. If not then maybe a local internet cafe could be your cup of tea. A good set of headphones to block out outer sounds can be useful aswell. Listening to classical music is seen as beneficial while studying or doing work.
